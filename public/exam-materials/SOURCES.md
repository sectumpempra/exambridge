# Official exam material snapshots

These PDFs are visually verified snapshots of the official documents linked in
the Exam Overview catalog. They are used only for the in-site preview because
some awarding-body servers block iframe embedding. Each material card keeps its
original awarding-body URL as the canonical source.

Snapshots are replaced only after the candidate diff has been reviewed and
approved.

The July 2026 expansion adds official snapshots for Cambridge 0606, 0607 and
9231; Pearson 4PM1 and 4MB1; the Pearson January 2027 IAL timetable; and the
Summer 2027 China R-paper timetable. Pearson IAL Mathematics, Further
Mathematics and Pure Mathematics deliberately share the same official
specification and formula-book snapshots.

The July 2026 science and computing expansion adds 15 course groups: Cambridge
0610, 0620, 0625, 0478, 9700, 9701, 9702 and 9618; Pearson International GCSE
4BI1, 4CH1, 4PH1 and 4CP0; and Pearson IAL Biology, Chemistry and Physics. The
catalog keeps the awarding-body PDF URL as canonical, stores only the documents
needed for syllabus, timetable and supplied-reference preview, and monitors
future Cambridge syllabuses before their activation window.

The Pearson UK mathematics catalog also includes 8MA0 AS Mathematics using the
official Issue 3 (October 2025) specification. Its in-site specification preview
is expected at `pearson-uk-8ma0-spec-issue3.pdf` in the persistent material
store; the PDF is deliberately not tracked in Git.
