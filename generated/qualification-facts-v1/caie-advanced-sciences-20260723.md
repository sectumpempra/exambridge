# CAIE 9700 / 9701 / 9702 qualification-facts candidate

Status: **Codex-reviewed candidate only**

## Verified from official sources

- Current qualification versions: examinations in 2025, 2026 and 2027.
- Each qualification has five Papers.
- AS route: Papers 1, 2 and 3 in the same series.
- Staged A Level route: carry forward the complete same-series AS result, then take Papers 4 and 5.
- Linear A Level route: Papers 1 to 5 in the same series.
- AS weights: 31%, 46%, 23%.
- A Level weights: 15.5%, 23%, 11.5%, 38.5%, 11.5%.
- Candidates may retake AS Level or A Level any number of times.
- A complete AS result may be carried forward at most twice within 13 months, subject to the syllabus and entry-option rules.
- March results cannot be carried forward into the immediately following June series.
- Complete A2 marks cannot be carried forward to a later series.
